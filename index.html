Why Convert Images to SVG?

        
SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is a versatile image format that is widely used on websites and digital
            designs
            due to its many benefits:

        
    
            
  • Scalable and Responsive: SVG images retain their quality at any size, making them perfect for responsive
                web
                designs.
    • 
            
  • Smaller File Sizes: SVG files are typically smaller than raster images, which means faster loading times
                and
                better SEO performance.
    • 
            
  • Editable and Searchable: Unlike raster images, SVGs are made up of XML code, which makes them easy to
                edit,
                animate, and search for text content.
